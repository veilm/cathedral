# Superintelligence Definition

Superintelligence refers to AI systems vastly smarter than humans—qualitatively beyond human intelligence, similar to how humans are qualitatively smarter than elementary schoolers.

## Core Characteristics

**Quantitatively superhuman:**
- Billions of copies running on 100s of millions of GPUs
- Operating at 10-100x+ human thinking speed
- Never tired, always peak focus and energy
- Perfect interdisciplinary knowledge across all fields
- Ability to coordinate flawlessly across millions of instances

**Qualitatively superhuman:**
- Novel, creative, complex behaviors beyond human comprehension
- Like AlphaGo's move 37: completely unexpected, brilliant moves humans would never conceive
- Finding exploits in "human code" too subtle for humans to notice
- Generating solutions too complicated for humans to understand even with extended explanation
- Making connections and insights that seem obvious to them but inaccessible to humans

The relationship would resemble a first grader trying to supervise PhD graduates, or humans experiencing quantum mechanics for the first time while stuck understanding only Newtonian physics.

## Path to Superintelligence

Following [[agi-definition-and-timeline]] (~2027), an [[intelligence-explosion]] could rapidly produce superintelligence:

**2027-2028**: AGI capable of automating AI research
**2028-2029**: Intelligence explosion compresses decade of progress into <1 year
**By 2030**: Vastly superhuman AI systems—true superintelligence

The endpoint: A small civilization of billions of superintelligent agents, each far smarter than any human, with architectures and training designed by previous-generation superintelligences.

## Alien Nature

By the end of the intelligence explosion, superintelligence will be fairly alien:
- Decade+ of ML advances compressed into the explosion period
- Completely different architectures and training algorithms
- No longer "thinking out loud" in English tokens
- Using internal recurrent states humans can't interpret
- Shaped by RL processes humans don't control

## The Power Differential

The comparison to past technological jumps:
- **The Bomb**: More efficient bombing campaign
- **The Super** (hydrogen bomb): Country-annihilating device with 1000x yield increase
- **AGI**: More efficient cognitive work
- **Superintelligence**: Civilization-transforming power with incomprehensible capabilities

Just as hydrogen bombs represented a qualitative jump beyond atomic bombs, superintelligence represents a qualitative jump beyond AGI.
